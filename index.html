About EcoLens

EcoLens is a lightweight, AI-powered web application that helps users scan everyday objects to estimate their carbon footprint. Our mission is to raise sustainability awareness through interactive technology.

Using advanced computer vision and voice interaction, EcoLens makes understanding environmental impact accessible to everyone.
